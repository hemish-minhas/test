Team COVID Overflow

We are a group of undergraduates students from University of Calgary and Mount Royal University. As we all go about these uncertain times, our school keeps us updated with new COVID cases on-campus through multiple emails. We noticed that the emails were repetitive and at some point most students were not paying much attention to them. Our solution to this is a website where information about each universities' COVID cases is displayed in a convenient manner so that students can easily browse through and view the current active case number in their school. This reduces the amount of email digging and searching through pages, that may not be user friendly, students needs to do to be informed and to make safe decisions.
